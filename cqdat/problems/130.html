Problem 130: Basketball Score

This practice problem was made by CodeRams Co-President Xavier Plourde
This problem has a difficulty of 5



Your favorite basketball team is playing in an important game, and you would like to determine if your team won or lost, and how close the game was.
In this problem, write a computer program to find the result of a basketball game given the two teams' scores.

Program Input


The first line of the input will contain a positive integer M less than 200, representing your favorite team's score at the end of the game

The second line of the input will contain a positive integer N less than 200, not equal to M, representing the opposing team's score at the end of the game

Example Input:


67
63

Program Output


Given the two teams scores, print the following
"Blowout Win" if your team won by 10 points or more
"Close Win" if your team won by less than 10 points
"Close Loss" if your team lost by less than 10 points
"Blowout Loss" if your team lost by 10 points or more
There are never any ties in basketball (because games go into overtime), so you don't have to worry about the numbers being equal.

Example Output:


Close Win
