CodeQuest 2019

  CodeQuest is an annual competition run by Lockheed Martin. CodeQuest 2019 will occur on April 27th, 2019. The CodeRams won three trophies at CodeQuest 2019, with the Codebusters taking first place in the advanced category, and Team Ben and Team Name Generator placing 2nd and 3rd in the novice category, respectively.

JD Coding invitational 2019

  The JD coding invitational competition is a local CNY computer science competition of high schools. 2019 is the first year of this competition, with hopefully many more to come in the future. We welcome any interested schools, and aim to provide a fun and competitive environment for all participants.

CodeQuest 2018

  CodeQuest is an annual competition run by Lockheed Martin. CodeQuest 2018 occurred on April 21, 2018. The CodeRams club sent four advanced teams to CodeQuest, with the Omega Rams team placing 2nd place at the Owego, NY site with 245 points.
