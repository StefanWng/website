CodeQuest 2018

  CodeQuest is an annual competition run by Lockheed Martin. CodeQuest 2018 occurred on April 21, 2018. The CodeRams club sent four advanced teams to CodeQuest, with the Omega Rams team placing 2nd place at the Owego, NY site with 245 points.
