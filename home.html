Who are we?

CodeRams is the computer science club of Jamesville Dewitt High School. Unlike many computer science based clubs, we place a heavy focus on involvement and success in competitions. We participate in the local Lockheed Martin CodeQuest competition, hosted in Owego NY. In addition, this year(2019), we are hosting a local coding invitational to increase regional participation in computer science. On top of our competition focus, we also work to provide computer science tutorials, advice, and ideas for all interested students at Jamesville Dewitt. During our weekly meetings on friday, we usually teach the club members a new coding skill, which will help them in their personal projects, and in the competitions. We encourage anybody interested in computer science to attend one of our meetings in B05.
